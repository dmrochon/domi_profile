Education

What I know so far.

I've learned so much from so many places. I started school at St. Peter Chanel School from Pre-K to 3rd grade. Then I went to St. Ann Catholic School for 4th grade and graduated in the 7th grade. I shortly attended Cabrini High School for 8th grade until Hurricane Katrina hit. During the displacement, I went to Ascension Catholic Middle School for the rest of 8th grade. The next year, I attended Mount St. Mary Catholic High School in Oaklahoma for 9th grade. A year later, I was able to return to and graduate from Cabrini High School. For my freshman year of college, the University of Louisiana Lafayette was my home. Lastly, I graduated from Loyola University New Orleans in 2015.
