Heart Container Valentine Sketches

A Legend of Zelda valentine for my boyfriend.

Watercolor is one of my favorite mediums. I use pencil and pen to make rough sketches. After the ink dries, I use a light table to trace over the inked sheet on a new piece paper with markers or colored pencils. Lastly, I apply the watercolor itself. I always save the inked paper so I can make another composition with them.
